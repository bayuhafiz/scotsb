Sauchiehall Street- Prime high street space with 24 rooms offered as student accommodation.

Sauchiehall Street is one of Glasgow’s most prominent shopping street alongside Buchannan Street and Argyle Street. Sauchiehall Street is a well-established for its restaurant thoroughfare and stellar bar scene. The Glasgow City Council is in the midst of redeveloping this site into a pedestrian high street walk focused on offering the best entertainment that Glasgow has to offer.

Scotsbridge Holdings acquired 387 Sauchiehall Street with a view of repositioning the property into a mixed commercial and residential site for student accommodation. The property currently has 16 rooms on the 2nd and 3rd floor being rented out to students. The 1st floor is to be converted into an additional 8 rooms, while the ground floor and basement will be leased out to an anchor tenant in the food and beverage industry.
